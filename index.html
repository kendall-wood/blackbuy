BlackBuy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2024
Effective Date: December 21, 2024

Overview

BlackBuy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Anonymous Usage Data

  • What: Daily scan count (number only)
  • Purpose: Enforce fair usage limits (10 scans per day)
  • Storage: Local device only, automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Sharing: Never shared with third parties

Anonymous User Identifier

  • What: Cryptographically hashed anonymous ID
  • Purpose: Associate usage limits with your app installation
  • Storage: Encrypted in device keychain
  • Sharing: Never shared with third parties

Camera Text Recognition

  • What: Text recognized from camera when scanning products
  • Purpose: Identify products and find Black-owned alternatives
  • Storage: Processed in memory only, never stored
  • Sharing: Never shared with third parties

Information We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Biometric data or stored camera images
  • ❌ Social media or contact information
  • ❌ Cross-app usage or behavioral tracking
  • ❌ Analytics or crash reporting data
  • ❌ Search history or browsing patterns

How We Use Information

  1. Rate Limiting: Anonymous usage counting to ensure fair access (10 scans per day)
  2. Product Search: OCR text processing to find product alternatives
  3. App Functionality: Basic app preferences and settings

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT share any data with:

  • Third-party companies
  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Social media platforms
  • Government agencies (except as required by law)

Data Security

  • All data stored using iOS Keychain encryption
  • Network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Automatic data cleanup and deletion
  • No cloud storage or external databases
  • Hardware-backed security on supported devices

Your Privacy Rights

Data Access

You can view all data we have about you (very minimal) through the app's Privacy Settings.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through:

  • App Settings > Privacy > Reset All Data
  • Deleting the app (removes all data)

Data Portability

You can export your data (minimal anonymous usage statistics) through the Privacy Settings.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

BlackBuy does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app:

  • Uses only anonymous identifiers
  • Collects no personal information
  • Has no social features or communication
  • Requires no account creation

International Privacy Laws

GDPR Compliance (EU)

  • Lawful Basis: Legitimate interest for app functionality
  • Data Minimization: Only essential data collected
  • Right to Access: Available through Privacy Settings
  • Right to Deletion: Available through Privacy Settings
  • Right to Portability: Available through Privacy Settings

CCPA Compliance (California)

  • Personal Information: We collect no personal information as defined by CCPA
  • Sale of Data: We do not sell any data
  • Right to Know: Fully disclosed in this policy
  • Right to Delete: Available through Privacy Settings

Data Retention

  • Scan Counts: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • User ID: Retained until app deletion
  • App Preferences: Retained until app deletion
  • OCR Text: Immediately deleted after processing
  • Search Queries: Immediately deleted after processing

Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notification
  • Updated policy date

  • Response Time: Within 7 business days
